Privacy Policy for PDFspliter

Your privacy is important to us. It is PDFspliter's policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect from you in our application.

1. Information We Collect

Our application, PDFspliter, is designed to work offline. It does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information about you. All PDF files and images you process with the application remain on your device.

2. Application Permissions

The application may request the following permissions:

  • Storage Access (Read and Write): This permission is necessary for the application to read the files (PDFs, JPGs) you select and save the processed files to your device.

3. Usage Data and Analytics

We do not use any third-party analytics tools to collect data about how you use the application. No information about your activities in the application is sent to us or to third parties.

4. Service Providers

We do not employ third-party companies and individuals in our application.

5. Security

Since we do not collect your data, there is no risk of data exposure on our part. The security of your files on your device depends on the general security measures of your Android device.

6. Links to Other Sites

Our application does not contain links to other sites.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for anyone under the age of 13. Since we do not collect personal information, we do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, we advise you to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

This policy is effective as of 12/25/2025.
